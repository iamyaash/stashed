# blog

## Template for naming the posts or other contents as well!
1. **Montly Report: `m1-2025.md`**
- `m1`...`m12` - `January` to `December`.
- Title: `Monthly Report: Jan 2025`

2. **Quarterly Report: `q1-2025.md`**
- `q1-2024` - `January, February & March on 2025`.
- `q2-2024` - `April, May & June on 2025`.
- `q3-2024` - `July, August & September on 2025`.
- `q4-2024` - `October, November & December on 2025`.
- Title: `Quarterly Report: Q1 2025`

3. **Weekly Report: `w1-m1-2025.md`**
- `w1`...`w4` - `Week 1 to 4`
- `q1`...`q4` - `Quarter Year 1 to 4`
- Title: `Weekly Report: w1 Jan 2024`
